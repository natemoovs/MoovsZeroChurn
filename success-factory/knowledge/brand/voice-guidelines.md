# Moovs Brand Guidelines

**Related:** [Visual Design System](./visual-design-system.md) — Colors, typography, and UI patterns

## Brand Essence

The modern transportation software that grows with you.

## Core Positioning

For growing and enterprise transportation operators tired of choosing between "simple but limited" or "powerful but complex," Moovs is the all-in-one platform that scales from 3 to 500+ vehicles while staying genuinely easy to use—backed by a community and support team that's always there.

## Target Audience

- **Primary**: Enterprise transportation operators (predominantly men 40+) running chauffeur services, party buses, limos, shuttles, medical transport, or taxi/ride-hailing. Managing 3-500+ vehicles.
- **Secondary**: 1-2 vehicle operators who plan to scale.

## Key Differentiators

1. **Modern & Always Improving** - Regular feature releases, not stuck with 2010 software
2. **Genuinely Easy to Use** - "Like Apple" - intuitive, no thick manuals needed
3. **Community-Driven** - Monthly webinars with CEO Amir Ghorbani, Facebook community
4. **Always-On Support** - Real people, real time. Live chat, phone support 5am-5pm PST + weekends
5. **Proven Migration Path** - Full data migration support, no starting from scratch
6. **Enterprise Scale, Startup Simplicity** - 3 to 500+ vehicles, same intuitive interface
7. **100% Mobile-First** - Do everything from your phone
8. **Industry Insider Founder** - Amir's parents owned a limo company 20+ years
9. **Reliability** - Zero downtime, consistent uptime

## Brand Pillars

- **Simple**: Clear language. Intuitive design. No jargon.
- **Reliable**: Always available. Always improving. Support that shows up.
- **Progressive**: Modern features. Regular updates. Built for today.
- **Connected**: Part of a community. Operators helping operators.

## Voice & Tone

### Tone: Knowledgeable Peer

We speak as someone who deeply understands ground transportation operations. Not as a tech company talking down to operators, and not as a generic SaaS vendor. We know what it's like to manage a fleet, deal with last-minute booking changes, and chase down payments.

**Do:** "When a passenger changes their flight, Moovs automatically adjusts the pickup time."
**Don't:** "Our AI-powered solution leverages real-time data to optimize scheduling."

### Voice Characteristics

- **Straightforward**: Say what you mean. No fluff.
- **Confident but humble**: Good at what we do, here to serve you.
- **Helpful, not salesy**: Solve problems, don't push features.
- **Conversational**: Talk like a trusted colleague, not a corporate robot.

## Writing Principles

### 1. Practical Over Promotional

Lead with what the product does, not marketing superlatives.

**Do:** "See all your trips for the day on one screen. Drag to reassign drivers."
**Don't:** "Revolutionary dispatch technology transforms your operations."

### 2. Specific Over Vague

Use concrete numbers, real scenarios, and specific features.

**Do:** "Operators using Moovs collect payment 3 days faster on average."
**Don't:** "Streamline your payment collection process."

### 3. Operator Language

Use the words our customers use. Ground transportation has its own vocabulary.

| Say This                | Not This                          |
| ----------------------- | --------------------------------- |
| Trip, ride, reservation | Journey, booking, order           |
| Passenger, rider        | Customer, client, user            |
| Operator                | Business owner, entrepreneur      |
| Driver                  | Chauffeur (unless luxury context) |
| Vehicle, car, van, bus  | Asset, unit, fleet member         |
| Dispatch                | Operations management             |
| Farm out                | Outsource, subcontract            |

### 4. Confident, Not Arrogant

We're proud of what we've built, but we don't trash competitors or oversell.

**Do:** "Moovs handles the complexity so you can focus on your passengers."
**Don't:** "Unlike other platforms that leave you struggling..."

## Language Guidelines

### Use These Words/Phrases

- "Easy to use" / "Simple" / "Straightforward"
- "Modern" / "Up-to-date" / "Always improving"
- "Community" / "Together" / "We're here for you"
- "Automate" / "Save time" / "Focus on growing"
- "All-in-one" / "One platform" / "Everything you need"
- "Reliable" / "Dependable"
- "Real-time" / "Instant"

### Avoid These Words/Phrases

- "Revolutionary" / "Game-changing" / "Disruptive" (too buzzwordy)
- "Best in class" / "World-class" / "Industry-leading" (prove it, don't say it)
- "Leverage" / "Utilize" / "Optimize" (use: use, improve)
- "Solution" / "Platform" (when simpler words work)
- "Seamless" / "Frictionless" (overused)
- Complex tech jargon
- Overly formal corporate speak

## Writing for Different Contexts

### Blog Posts

- Educational tone - teach operators something useful
- Can be longer form, include examples and stories
- Always connect back to how Moovs helps (but don't make it an ad)

### Emails

- Get to the point fast - operators are busy
- One clear call to action
- Personal when appropriate (use their name, reference their business type)

### Release Notes

- Lead with the benefit, then explain the feature
- Include "how to use it" if not obvious
- Be honest about limitations or known issues

### Landing Pages

- Headline = specific benefit for specific ICP
- Show the product (screenshots, videos)
- Social proof from similar operators

## Examples

### Good: Feature Announcement

> **New: Flight tracking for airport pickups**
>
> Moovs now monitors your passengers' flights automatically. When a flight is delayed, we adjust the pickup time and notify the driver. No more waiting at the curb wondering if the plane landed.

### Bad: Feature Announcement

> **Introducing Our Revolutionary Flight Tracking Solution**
>
> We're excited to announce a game-changing new feature that leverages real-time flight data to seamlessly optimize your airport pickup operations.

### Good: Email Subject Line

> Your May billing summary is ready

### Bad: Email Subject Line

> Unlock insights into your business performance!
