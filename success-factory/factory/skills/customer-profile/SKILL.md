## Questions

### businessName: What is your business name?
Examples:
- Acme Corp
- Smith's Moving Company
- Elite Relocations LLC

### industry: What industry best describes your business?
Examples:
- Residential moving
- Commercial moving
- Specialty/white glove
- Full-service (residential + commercial)

### teamSize: How many people are on your team?
Examples:
- Just me
- 2-5 employees
- 6-15 employees
- 16+ employees

### topChallenge: What's your biggest challenge with customer retention right now?
Examples:
- Customers don't come back for repeat moves
- Low referral rates
- No system to stay in touch with past customers
- Don't know why customers leave
